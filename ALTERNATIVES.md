# ✅ Privacy-Respecting Alternatives to Google

A curated guide to open-source, secure, and privacy-conscious replacements for Google services. These tools reduce tracking and put **you** in control of your data. Start with one, then gradually switch more.

---

## 🔍 Search Engines

**Replace Google Search with:**

* **[SearXNG](https://searx.space)** – Self-hosted metasearch engine that combines results from multiple sources anonymously.
* **[DuckDuckGo](https://duckduckgo.com)** – Popular search engine with no tracking or profiling.
* **[Kagi](https://kagi.com)** – Premium ad-free search engine with a focus on quality and privacy.
* **[Whoogle](https://github.com/benbusby/whoogle-search)** – Self-hosted proxy for Google Search without the tracking.
* **[Brave Search](https://search.brave.com)** – Privacy-focused engine with its own independent index.
